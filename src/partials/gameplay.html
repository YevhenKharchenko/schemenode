How To Play

Run, jump, survive

  • Start Running

    Move forward automatically through each level.

  • Jump Over Obstacles

    Tap at the right moment to avoid hazards and gaps.

  • Defeat or Avoid Enemies

    Time your movement to stay safe.

  • Collect Items

    Gather coins and bonuses along the way.

  • Reach the Goal

    Complete the level by arriving at the finish line.
