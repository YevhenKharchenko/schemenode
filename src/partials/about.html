About the Game

Small hero. Big adventure.

In Super Pug Story, you play as this adorable little pug who's gotta run and jump through all these crazy levels - some are pretty tricky!
It's all about getting your timing right and exploring around, but don't worry - it keeps things fun and not too serious.
As you go further, the levels throw more stuff at you - new enemies, trickier jumps, that sort of thing.
