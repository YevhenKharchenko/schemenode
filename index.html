About the Game

A casual destruction game built around satisfying physics

The core gameplay revolves around moving a black hole across different environments while absorbing objects to grow larger and unlock new challenges.
Every level introduces new layouts, object types, and destruction mechanics that keep gameplay feeling active and rewarding.
The overall experience should feel smooth, funny, and visually satisfying without becoming complicated.

Features

Built to hook you with quick, bite-sized sessions

  • Physics-Based Destruction

    Stuff bounces around realistically as it gets sucked in - it's pretty cool to watch.

  • Progressive Gameplay Flow

    Levels gradually introduce larger environments and new mechanics.

  • Bright Casual Visuals

    The bright, clean graphics make it easy to see what's happening, even when everything's going nuts.

  • Fast Mobile Sessions

    Gameplay is designed around short, satisfying rounds.

  • Upgrade & Growth System

    Players increase hole size, speed, and absorption power over time.
